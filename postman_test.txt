### Postman 测试集合 —— 需求流程
### 变量: base_url = http://localhost:8080


### 1. 新增需求（自动启动流程）
POST {{base_url}}/requirement/add
Content-Type: application/json

{
    "requirementNo": "REQ001",
    "requirementType": "IDC",
    "requirementName": "测试需求-座舱升级",
    "priority": "高",
    "description": "这是一个测试需求"
}


### 2. 查询流程状态
GET {{base_url}}/requirement/flow/state?requirementNo=REQ001


### 3. 提交审批（草稿 → 待审批）
POST {{base_url}}/requirement/flow/send
Content-Type: application/json

{"requirementNo":"REQ001","event":"submit"}


### 4. 审批通过（待审批 → 评审中）
POST {{base_url}}/requirement/flow/send
Content-Type: application/json

{"requirementNo":"REQ001","event":"approve"}


### 5. 评审通过（评审中 → 验收中）
POST {{base_url}}/requirement/flow/send
Content-Type: application/json

{"requirementNo":"REQ001","event":"review_pass"}


### 6. 验收通过（验收中 → 已完成）
POST {{base_url}}/requirement/flow/send
Content-Type: application/json

{"requirementNo":"REQ001","event":"accept"}


### 7. 驳回测试（回到草稿）
POST {{base_url}}/requirement/flow/send
Content-Type: application/json

{"requirementNo":"REQ001","event":"reject"}


### 8. 查看流程日志
GET {{base_url}}/requirement/logs?requirementNo=REQ001
